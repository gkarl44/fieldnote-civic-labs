Privacy

Website measurement is limited and practical.

Field Note Civic Labs may use privacy-conscious analytics and server request logs to understand whether the site is discoverable, which pages are requested, and whether search or AI crawlers are reading public files such as robots.txt, sitemap.xml, llms.txt, and site-index.json.

Logs may include request time, requested path, referrer, user agent, country, autonomous system number, network organization, and broad bot or crawler signals when available. The site does not use advertising trackers, cross-site tracking cookies, or user profiles.

Email inquiries are handled through the address shown on the contact section. Do not send sensitive personal, legal, or confidential material unless there is an agreed reason to do so.
